About YGMM

Learn about our vision, mission, history, and leadership team

Our Story

The Young Gospel Ministers Movement (YGMM) was founded in 2025 by a young man of God who was led by the spirit of God assisted by a group of young ministers who saw the need for a dedicated community for emerging gospel workers.

Our focus is on equipping the next generation of ministers with both theological depth and practical ministry skills. We believe that effective gospel ministry requires both sound doctrine and compassionate service.

Today, YGMM serves young ministers across denominational lines, providing resources, training, and community to help them fulfill their calling.

Our Mission

To preach the pure Gospel of Christ to Humanity Leading into Conversion and Disciple them into the full stature of Christ.

Our Vision

To be a vibrant (interdeniminational) Movement that will inspire and equip Christian youth,To Embrace and spread the pure Gospel of Christ,Pursuing righteousiness in all they do and becoming agents of change in the great and last revival

Free Membership Benefits

YGMM is committed to supporting young ministers without financial barriers. Our membership is completely free to ensure everyone can access the resources they need.

COMPLETELY FREE MEMBERSHIP
  • Complete Resource Access: Full library of training materials, sermon outlines, and ministry tools at no cost
  • Community Networking: Connect with fellow young ministers across denominations and regions
  • Mentorship Matching: Be paired with an experienced minister for guidance and support
  • Event Participation: Access to most YGMM conferences, workshops, and retreats
  • Monthly Training: Live webinars on relevant ministry topics
  • Prayer Network: Join our prayer community and have our team pray for your ministry
  • Ministry Support: Guidance for challenging ministry situations and decisions
  • Leadership Development: Training to help you grow as a spiritual leader
